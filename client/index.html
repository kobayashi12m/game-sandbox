Celestial Game Online

Free real-time multiplayer browser game. Command your celestial system and battle other players.

How to Play

  • Move toward your cursor or finger
  • Click or tap to eject satellites and attack
  • Collect dropped satellites to grow stronger
  • Destroy enemy cores to score points

Features

  • Real-time multiplayer combat via WebSocket
  • Orbital mechanics — satellites orbit your core
  • Works on desktop and mobile — no download required
  • Free to play, no sign-up needed
